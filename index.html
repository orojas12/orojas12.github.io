About Me

My name is Oscar, and I'm from El Paso, Texas. Technology has been a big part of my life since I was very young (I had my first computer at age 5). At a young age, I had a strong interest in what made computers work, and over the years programming came naturally and ignited a spark inside me.

To this day, this burning passion continues to push me to pursue a career in this field. I graduated from El Paso Community College in May 2021 with an Associate in Computer Programming. I have experience in front-end web development, back-end web APIs and databases, and a small amount in Android development.

My current goal is to continually improve my skills in software development and become an accomplished developer in the industry. Please take a look at my projects to see my current progress. :)
